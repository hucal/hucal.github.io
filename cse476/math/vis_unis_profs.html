This is a D3 visualization of the large family tree composed of doctoral advisor-student relationships and the institutions the doctors served at. Data was scraped off Wikipedia using Scrapy

Blue nodes are universities/institutions and red nodes are people with PhDs drawn on the geometric centroid of the institutions they or their advisors served at. Hence the nodes floating over the ocean. Red curves are between the advisor and student, blue lines are between a person and institution. Datasets, color scheme and map projection can be changed, and the search bar highlights nodes on submission.

Try searching for famous names and universities like Gauss, Harvard or Los Alamos. Also view clusters located over the ocean and certain towns.

Try the data/Zygmund.json dataset if the larger datasets can't be navigated comfortably.
